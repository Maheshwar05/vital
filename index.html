    • What’s Included in a Website Design & Development Project?

    Building a standout website takes expertise, experience, and a time-tested process. And, as a full-service digital marketing agency, we bake in the functionality and best practices that enable your site to drive the business results you need.

    Plus we’re wicked fun to work with. Here’s what to expect:

    Getting to Know You

    We start every web project with a full discovery process to uncover insights about your brand, your goals and challenges, your audience, and your competition. Once we have what we need to define your site’s strategy, it’s off to the races.

    Time-tested, Future-focused Process

    We break our process into distinct phases that keep everyone on track, on budget, and on the same page — and we make sure learning and continuous improvement is part of the plan, so innovation is no accident.

    Frequent Touchpoints

    Following an initial all-hands-on-deck kickoff call, your dedicated account manager and strategist will meet with you weekly to share updates and address any questions, concerns, or new ideas. Meanwhile, a talented team of designers, developers, and copywriters is hard at work bringing your vision to life.

    Our Approach

    Depending on your goals and functionality requirements, your timeline, and your budget, we will recommend one of two approaches to designing and developing your website.

    Custom Websites

    Development is tailored to the specific requirements and scale of the project. We perform in-depth discovery and research to understand your unique business needs and objectives. We then identify custom features, functionalities, and integrations to meet a range of complex demands.
